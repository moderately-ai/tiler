Accepted ADR 0039 after compiler/tensor/user-system precedent review: fixed-width integer add/sub/mul use explicit extensible overflow-specialized families. Initial recognized semantics are wrapping, saturating, checked, and widening; required-no-overflow is a proof/runtime-validation obligation, never poison. Wrapping may be an ergonomic frontend default but is explicit in canonical IR.
